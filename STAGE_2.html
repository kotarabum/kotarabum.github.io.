Stage 2 Brief “Temple of meditation in alternative future” proposing the future of Fleet Street in next 2000 year’s, it can be predicted that current problems and events occurring around the world and down to the site will permanently change our mindset: generation suffering from race and gender inequalities, COVID-19, climate change, urbanisation and economic crisis may lead to human depression. the temple of meditation is designed for local people to help reach nirvana on the mental level and fulfil the universe with somebody or alone to overcome anxiety or simply have a break after hard day. Construction method combines the feeling of vertical forest that consist of large variety of plant species and virtual reality system into collaborative space of calmness and endless existence of a space of continues growth. Project integrate variety of conceptual models and sketches of progress, however the final outcome model has set the main sequences and elements of the proposal that essential to keep building running for long time frames
